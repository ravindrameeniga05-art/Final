Our Vision

YT Overseas is an international consultancy offering assistance to students wishing to pursue further education overseas and career advice that enables people to make choices for advancing a successful career.
We pride ourselves in providing efficient and ethically driven student counselling as well as employability advice on the international platform that helps prospective students from everywhere to pursue higher education abroad and to further carer prospects.

Our professional and experienced team of advisers will offer comprehensive service assisting you with researching and applying for the chosen course, identifying the right university to meet your needs, applying for visa, making travel arrangements and settling into the university. We specialize in facilitating access to higher education in the UK, Ireland, Canada, USA and Australia.

If you are considering education overseas, rest assured you are at the right place. ECCHO offers tailored consultation services to prospective students and job seekers guiding them at every step of the way. Our services start with a friendly chat with one of team members to explore what you are looking for and identify the right place to begin your journey to further education and training. YT Overseas is a UK registered company with offices in the UK and India. We offer our services face-to-face, over the telephone or through chat. So, if you are looking for tailored services to meet your needs, we are just a phone call away.

We are completely oriented to doing our best for our clientele within the constraints that may prevail in each case, be it educational, immigration, or language requirements. Our student success rate is 98% compared to an international overall of 60%. YT Overseas is unique in its services as it is student centered in its approach and in its operation.

So, why not sign-up for a free consultation that will help you find the right path whether you are looking for a language course, vocational training, undergraduate or postgraduate courses and internships or simply someone to talk through your options. Our specialist services are available for applying to a high-ranking university mainly in Medicine, Art and Design.

We Also Provide IELTS Guidance With Training To Ensure That The Target Score Is Achieved.
